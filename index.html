We are Top Gun Robotics!

We are a FIRST Robotics Competiton team based at Eastlake High School in Sammamish, WA. We teach high school students STEM skills by building an up to 6 foot, 120 pound robot for participating in robotics competitions every year. From building to programming to fundraising to leadership, everything is student designed, student built, and student run.

How to Get Involved

There are many ways to get involved with Top Gun! From joining the team as a student, volunteering to mentor as a professional adult, to simply helping us reach our fundraising goals, there are so many ways for you to help out.

Students

Anyone can join our team. No matter what school, grade, or experience level, you are able to join. We teach the basics and have you gain experience through hands-on work.

Join the team!

Mentors

Mentors are parent volunteers that help teach our team the skills we need for the robot as well as life. No matter what profession, from engineering to buisness, all sorts of skills are needed -- we can learn from anything you have to teach!

Donations

To continue operations of the team, we must pay a lot of expenses, including transportation to competitions, parts, and competition entry fees. As such, any contributions helps our students learn. Donations are tax deductable!
