FIRST Launch 2019

The official challenge themes for the 2019 season of all FIRST programs has been revealed, and teams this year are going to space! For the first time, all four programs, K-12, all share the common theme of reaching for the stars and exploring the depths of space.
For 30 years, FIRST® has inspired millions of young people to reach for the stars.

Blast off with FIRST LAUNCH 2019 and find out what’s possible when we dream big and work together to inspire the next generation of innovators, technology leaders, and changemakers here on Earth – and beyond.
Source: FIRST

The 2019 FIRST Robotics Competition game is Destination: Deep Space!
